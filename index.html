Madagascar my country

Madagascar is a large island nation located off the southeastern coast of Africa in the Indian Ocean. It is famous for its unique biodiversity, with over 90% of its wildlife- such as lemurs and Baobab trees-found nowhere else on Earth. The island's landscapes range from rainforests and mountains to dry deserts and coral reefs. Culturally, Madagascar blends influences from Africa, Asia and Europe, reflected in its languages, cuisine and traditions.
