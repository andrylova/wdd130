Rapids! Make Memories!

Our mission is to deliver unforgettable river adventures that blend excitement, safety, and respect for nature. For over 30 years, our dedicated guides have helped families, friends, and explorers create memories that last a lifetime. We believe in teamwork, the thrill of the wild, and the joy that comes from embracing the great outdoors together.

History

Rapids! started floating the river in 1995. We have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30 years. We are a family-owned and operated business dedicated to providing you with the best experience possible.
