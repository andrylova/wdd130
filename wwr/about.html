History

Founded in the misty spring of 1987, our rafting company began as a small crew of river wanderers who believed the rapids spoke their own secret language. Over the years, our team grew from three fearless paddlers to a full family of adventure-seekers, guiding travelers through swirling waters, sunlit canyons, and currents that whisper tales older than time. Though much has changed, our mission remains the same: ride the wild, respect the river, and share the joy of the journey with everyone who dares to join us.
